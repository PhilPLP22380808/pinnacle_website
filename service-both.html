Other Costs to Consider

These are fees payable to third parties, not to Pinnacle:

Mortgage Arrangement Fees

Lender fees charged as part of your loan, including arrangement fees, booking fees, or survey/valuation fees.

Land Registry Registration Fees

The Government charge to process your purchase registration, paid to HM Land Registry.

Stamp Duty Land Tax

The transaction tax the Government charges on property transactions. Use the Government calculator as a starting point.

Leasehold Admin Fees (if applicable)

If there's a management company, they may charge extra admin fees payable directly to them.

Removal Costs

Moving costs including professional removals, packing materials, storage, or additional charges such as parking restrictions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really a fixed fee quote?

Yes. Our fee is a fully comprehensive, fixed and guaranteed price for our service. It never changes, even if complexities in the work are identified at a later date. We never charge you any more than we have quoted you here, upfront.

What happens if one of my transactions falls through?

We do not charge abortive costs. If either transaction is not successful for any reason, we do not charge you any abortive costs at all. We'll pick up where we left off with new properties.

How do you coordinate the chain?

As we handle both your sale and purchase, we can perfectly synchronise the two transactions, managing exchange and completion dates to ensure everything aligns. You'll have a single point of contact who understands both sides.

Who will I work with?

Once we have more information about your cases, we'll pair you with a lawyer best suited to your particular circumstances. You'll always have two dedicated, named persons to contact.
