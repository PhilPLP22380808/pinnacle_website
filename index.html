Our Guarantees

Our promises to you

In an industry full of unfair practices, we're committed to showing that the legal process can be a helpful stepping stone, not a painful hurdle.

01

No Move, No Fee

Commit to the legal process knowing that should your matter not complete, you won't be burdened with any outstanding legal fees. We only get paid once we've helped you achieve your goal.

Fixed Prices

No if's and but's — our pricing structure is crystal clear and you know exactly what you will pay us. No add-ons, no extra charges, just one fixed fee from the outset.

Communication, Communication, Communication

We guarantee updates at every stage of the process, as well as a constant line of communication. Same-day feedback is no problem for us — we're here to help.
