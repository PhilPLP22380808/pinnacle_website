Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really a fixed fee quote?

Yes. Our fee is a fully comprehensive, fixed and guaranteed price for our service. It never changes, even if complexities in the work are identified at a later date.

What happens if my sale falls through?

We do not charge abortive costs. If your transaction is not successful for any reason, we do not charge you any abortive costs at all. We'll pick up where we left off with a new buyer.

Who will I work with?

You'll be paired with a lawyer best suited to your particular circumstances. You'll always have two dedicated, named persons to contact, so you always have an immediate point of contact.
