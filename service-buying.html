Other Costs to Consider

These are fees payable to third parties, not to Pinnacle:

Mortgage Arrangement Fees

Lender fees charged as part of your loan, including arrangement fees, booking fees, or survey/valuation fees. Sometimes these can be added to the mortgage rather than paid upfront.

Land Registry Registration Fees

The Government charge to process your purchase registration. This is paid to HM Land Registry, not your conveyancer. We'll help you know the value once your file is open.

Stamp Duty Land Tax

The transaction tax the Government charges on property transactions. This can be highly complex — use the Government calculator as a starting point.

Leasehold Admin Fees (if applicable)

If there's a management company, they may charge extra admin fees payable directly to them. Any work related to this is included in our quote — we don't add handling fees.

Indemnity Insurance (if needed)

You may decide to solve small or low-risk legal issues with insurance. We can arrange this at no extra cost other than the policy itself, payable on completion.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really a fixed fee quote?

Yes. Our fee is a fully comprehensive, fixed and guaranteed price for our service. It never changes, even if complexities in the work are identified at a later date. We never charge you any more than we have quoted you here, upfront.

What happens if my transaction falls through?

We do not charge abortive costs. If your transaction is not successful for any reason — be that your decision, the decision of a third party, or any other reason — we do not charge you any abortive costs at all.

I've found a cheaper quote. What's the difference?

Very 'cheap' quotes often misrepresent the total costs by showing a cheap 'base' fee but making it up with add-on charges. There's a functional minimum that all Conveyancers have to charge — do you want to trust such an important purchase with a firm that chooses not to disclose true costs upfront?

Who will I work with?

Once we have more information about your case, we'll pair you with a lawyer best suited to your particular circumstances. You'll always have two dedicated, named persons to contact, so you always have an immediate point of contact.
